Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the power of the Elden Ring and become an Elden Lord in the Lands Between. In the Lands Between ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal, the Elden Ring, the source of the Erdtree, has been shattered. Marika's offspring, demigods all, claimed the shards of the Elden Ring known as the Great Runes, and the mad taint of their newfound strength triggered a war: The Shattering. A war that meant abandonment by the Greater Will. And now the guidance of grace will be brought to the Tarnished who were spurned by the grace of gold and exiled from the Lands Between. Ye dead who yet live, your grace long lost, follow the path to the Lands Between beyond the foggy sea to stand before the Elden Ring. And become the Elden Lord.

Ranni the Witch is an NPC in Elden Ring. Ranni the Witch is a mysterious figure who first introduces herself as Renna. Upon meeting her again at Three Sisters, she reveals her true name and eventually will recruit you into her servitude, sending you off in search of a hidden treasure. Godrick the Grafted is a Boss in Elden Ring. Godrick the Grafted is the ruler of Stormveil Castle, and one of the first demigods that players will fight. You don't need to initially fight Godrick the Grafted to progress into the next area of Elden Ring, and can instead opt to face him at a later part of the game. He drops the Remembrance of the Grafted when he is defeated.
"I command thee, kneel! I am the lord of all that is golden!"

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon is an NPC in Elden Ring. Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon is the last Carian Queen, and the NPC the player must use to respec. She can also be used to change the player's appearance. For her boss form that must be fought before gaining access to her NPC form, see Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon.
"Ah... Thou... Is it thy wish to be born anew? To become a sweeting, reborn of my beloved egg?

Starscourge Radahn is a Boss in Elden Ring. Starscourge Radahn is one of the most powerful demigods located in Caelid, where he can be accessed from Redmane Castle after activating the Radahn Festival by progressing far enough in Ranni's questline, or activating any site of grace in Altus Plateau. Defeating this Boss is mandatory for at least one ending of the game and to complete certain NPC questlines. However he does not necessarily have to be defeated to finish the game.
